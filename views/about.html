About Me

A developer and enthusiast of Lego Vintage Sets.


I'm Wanhua Wu. Currently studying at Seneca Polytechnic

My passion for these classic building blocks has given me a deep appreciation for the history and evolution of LEGO over the years. Through collecting and exploring these timeless sets, I immerse myself in the nostalgia and creativity that LEGO has inspired for generations. Join me on a journey through the fascinating world of vintage LEGO, where each set tells a unique story of innovation and imagination.
